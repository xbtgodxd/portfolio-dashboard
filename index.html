About this dashboard
Everything runs in your browser. There is no server and no account. Your positions, notes and history are stored in your browser's local storage, on your device — nothing you enter is ever uploaded.
The only network requests are price lookups. Live prices and exchange rates come from CoinGecko, Binance and Yahoo Finance. Those requests contain ticker symbols only — never amounts, balances or notes.
Back up regularly. Because your data lives in this browser, clearing browsing data erases it. The Backup button in the header downloads everything as a single file; Restore brings it back — on this machine or any other.
